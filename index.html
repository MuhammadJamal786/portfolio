About Me

Hi, I'm Jamal Khan. I'm a software engineer with 3 years of experience. I have a diverse skill set that includes programming in Python, C/C++, and working with machine language. My expertise extends to creating fully functional automated drones, autopilots, flight controllers, and drone mesh communication devices. I also have significant experience with electronics, including designing and fabricating PCBs in Altium, programming STM32 chips using ST-Link, and uploading programs to EEPROMs.

I have also worked as a full-stack web developer specializing in Laravel. My interests lie in making unique and cool things and experimenting with new technologies.
